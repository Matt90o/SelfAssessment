Knowledge of the theory of MHD

Knowledge of the theory of MHD refers to a body of generally accepted insights. Theoretical knowledge in general describes how the central concepts of a discipline are interrelated. It relates to the state-of-the-art knowledge within a scientific discipline.

MSc: has a thorough mastery of parts of the relevant fields extending to forefront of knowledge (latest theorems, methods, techniques and topical questions)
